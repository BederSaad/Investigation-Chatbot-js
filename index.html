Mystery Awaits

Unravel the Shadows


Yoshiko Nakamura's sudden and tragic death at Seiran University has sent shockwaves through the entire school community. Renowned for her intelligence, kindness, and promising future, Yoshiko's life was cruelly cut short when she was discovered lifeless in the computer lab, her face stained with blood and evidence of strangulation around her neck. Tasked with unraveling this intricate mystery is Detective James Taylor, a seasoned FBI Head and distinguished investigator known for his astuteness and unwavering determination.


As Detective Taylor delves into the case, it becomes evident that Yoshiko's demise was no accident but a deliberate act. His investigation leads him to scrutinize motives and analyze the complex web of relationships intertwining the victim and the suspects across campus. Five individuals emerge as prime suspects in Detective Taylor's inquiry: Mr. Tadashi, Kumi, Yuki, Fox, and April. Each holds crucial pieces of the puzzle, and Detective Taylor meticulously interrogates them to uncover the truth.


Kumi recounts her whereabouts, revealing her presence in the college library with her boyfriend Fox during the fateful hours. She highlights Yoshiko's demeanor and the tensions that occasionally brewed between them, shedding light on April's suspicious presence at the crime scene.


Yuki, reprimanded for previous violent behavior, shares his alibi, stating his obligation to academic research under Mr. Tadashi's supervision. He recalls encountering Fox, Yoshiko's close friend, during the night, drawing attention to April's recent altercation with the victim.


Mr. Tadashi, deeply saddened by Yoshiko's passing, recounts his activities and observations on the night of the murder, raising suspicions about Yuki's behavior. He discloses April's unique circumstances, indicating her struggle with schizophrenia and the unconventional therapy she undergoes.


Fox, Yoshiko's loyal friend, provides his testimony, detailing his presence in the library and the confrontation with Kumi. He describes the chilling moment when he witnesses April near the crime scene, prompting him to seek help for Yoshiko.


Through meticulous questioning and observation, Detective Taylor uncovers layers of deception, tension, and hidden truths among the suspects. As the investigation unfolds, the search for justice intensifies, leading to a dramatic climax where alliances are tested, secrets are exposed, and the fate of Yoshiko Nakamura hangs in the balance.
